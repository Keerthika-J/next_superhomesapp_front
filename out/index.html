01End To End ServiceBuilding approvals, architectural design, structural design, site execution, interiors and professional project management.
02In-house architects & engineersWe do not outsource any work. Our in house team of experienced architects and engineers will ensure your custom home construction is completed as planned
03Upfront Cost TransparencyOur pricing is flexible, transparent and based on your budget. You will receive detailed costing based on actual built up area calculations and specification selected.
04On-time CompletionYou will receive a detailed project schedule at the very beginning and we will ensure that the timelines are followed diligently till completion and handover. The timelines are documented in the agreements.
05Project TrackingYou will be given regular updates along with photographic evidence from site so that you do not have to visit the site everyday. All the drawings, invoices and documents related to your project will be made available in a single easy-to-access digital location.
06Quality ControlWe follow QuaSet; a thorough 350+ point quality control checklist to ensure that your home gets the top most level of workmanship using only branded material approved by you.
07WarrantiesWe will make sure you will never need to use these warranties. Just to give you peace of mind, we provide warranties on the following: 10 year structural warranty and 1 year seepage rectification from the time of completion.
